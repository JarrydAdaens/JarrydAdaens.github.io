Privacy Policy

A simple privacy policy for my apps and projects.

Effective date: May 19, 2026

Publisher: Jarryd Adaens

Overview

This site and the apps listed here are intended to be simple, static, and low maintenance. Individual app pages may include app-specific privacy notes where needed.

Data Collection

Unless an individual app page says otherwise, the apps listed on this site are not designed to collect, sell, rent, or share personal data.

Local Files and Device Data

Some desktop apps may read or write local files that you configure or choose while using the app. Those files remain on your device unless you separately use another tool or service to move, publish, back up, or sync them.

Data Leaving the Device

Unless an app-specific page says otherwise, app data is not uploaded to a publisher-operated cloud service. If you use separate tools such as IDEs, agents, extensions, cloud backup services, or source-control services, those tools are governed by their own privacy policies.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

The first version of the site and listed apps does not include publisher-operated analytics or crash reporting. Microsoft Store, Google Play, GitHub Pages, browsers, operating systems, or hosting providers may process their own diagnostic, security, or usage data under their own policies.

Support Requests

If you contact support, the information you choose to include in that request may be used to respond to your message and troubleshoot the issue.

Children

The apps and projects listed here are intended as developer, productivity, learning, or portfolio material and are not directed to children.

Changes

This policy may be updated as apps are published, store requirements change, or app-specific privacy notes are added. The effective date will be updated for material changes.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact details on the support page.
