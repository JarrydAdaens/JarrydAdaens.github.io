Privacy policy

Kung-Fu: Agentic IDE Helper

Effective date: May 19, 2026

Publisher: Jarryd Adaens

Overview

Kung-Fu: Agentic IDE Helper is a local-first desktop utility. It is designed to help organise files and artifacts in folders that you configure on your own device.

Personal Data

The app does not require an account and does not ask you to provide personal information. The app is not designed to collect, sell, rent, or share personal data.

Local Files

The app may read and write files in local folders that you configure or choose while using the app. Those files remain on your device unless you separately use another tool or service to move, publish, back up, or sync them.

Cloud Services and Sync

The app does not provide cloud synchronization and does not upload your workspace contents to a publisher-operated service.

Telemetry and Analytics

The app does not include publisher-operated telemetry or analytics. Microsoft Store, Windows, GitHub, web hosting, browser, network, or operating system services may process their own diagnostic, security, or usage data under their own privacy policies.

Generative AI

The app is not described as generating AI content inside the application. If you use separate IDEs, agents, extensions, models, or services alongside this app, those separate tools are governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

Children

The app is intended as a developer utility and is not directed to children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app changes or when store requirements change. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, use the contact options on the support page.
