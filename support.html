Support and Contact

A simple public support page for apps, projects, privacy questions, and store review contact details.

Support Email

jared.adeans.support@outlook.com.au

This is the working support address from the design document. It can be replaced before launch if the final address changes.

What To Include

  • The app or project name.
  • The app version, if available.
  • Your Windows, Android, browser, or platform version where relevant.
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected.
  • Any non-sensitive error message that helps explain the problem.

Privacy Questions

For privacy-related questions, include the app name and a brief description of the concern. The general policy is available at Privacy Policy.

No Contact Form

This first version does not include a backend, database, or contact form. Support is handled through email.
