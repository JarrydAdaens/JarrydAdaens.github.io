Before Requesting Help

  • Check that the folder you configured still exists and is accessible from Windows.
  • Check that your user account has permission to read and write the selected folder.
  • Restart the app after changing folder permissions or moving a workspace.
  • Include the app version, Windows version, and a short description of what you expected to happen.

Privacy Questions

The app is designed as a local-first utility. The current privacy policy is available at Privacy Policy.
